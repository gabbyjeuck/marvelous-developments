About Gabrielle

Why I created Marvelous Developments

The name "Marvelous Developments" is a heartfelt homage to my grandfather, Marvin Johnson. His name, Marvin, sparked the idea to create something "Marvelous" as a tribute to his influence on my life. He was not only a guiding light in my personal growth but also a source of inspiration as I embarked on my journey into the world of coding and technology. By naming my freelance platform after him, I aim to honor his memory and carry forward his legacy of inspiration and innovation. It's a reminder that every line of code and every project I complete is a step towards fulfilling the potential he saw in me.

Professional Journey

My adventure in the tech industry is anchored at Atlantic BT, where I contribute as a developer and application architect. Every project is an opportunity to explore new horizons and push the boundaries of what's possible.
